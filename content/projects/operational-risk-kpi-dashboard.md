# Operational Risk KPI Dashboard – Data Analytics

## Overview

A comprehensive Power BI dashboard that visualizes 50+ synthetic risk incidents by department, root cause, and severity, enabling leadership to conduct effective risk reviews and make data-driven mitigation decisions.

## The Challenge

Leadership teams lacked visibility into operational risk patterns across departments, making it difficult to:
- Prioritize mitigation efforts effectively
- Identify recurring risk patterns
- Allocate resources for risk management
- Track progress on risk reduction initiatives

## The Solution

### Comprehensive Risk Visualization
- Department-wise risk incident breakdown
- Root cause analysis with trend identification
- Severity level distribution and escalation patterns
- Time-series analysis for risk trend identification

### Interactive Dashboard Features
- Drill-down capabilities from high-level overview to detailed incidents
- Filtering by department, severity, time period, and root cause
- Real-time updates and automated refresh cycles
- Export capabilities for executive reporting

### KPI Tracking
- Risk incident frequency by department
- Average resolution time by severity level
- Cost impact analysis of risk incidents
- Mitigation effectiveness metrics

## Technical Implementation

### Tools & Technologies
